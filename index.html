Ferme Les Yeux is a scroll examining themes of introspection, self-discovery and the pursuit of inner peace. It intricately weaves together excerpts from the book Siddhartha by Hermann Hesse, leveraging its profound insights to delve into the fluidity of relationships. Through Siddhartha's journey, it navigates the ever-changing dynamics of human connections, illuminating the enduring nature of bonds and the profound impact they have on individual growth and understanding.
